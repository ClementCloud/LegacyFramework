Section I, Purpose & Definitions

Purpose, Principles & Core Definitions

The Sunshine Trust is a perpetual family capital system designed to preserve principal across generations while allocating only annual net yield for governance, compounding, and merit-based family benefits. It is not a vehicle for wealth consumption. It is a vehicle for wealth perpetuation and cultural infrastructure.

"Principal is sacred and protected. We only distribute the yield it generates. The Trust grows forever while providing annual benefits to family members."

🛡️
Capital Preservation First. Corpus is sacred and protected. It may not be invaded for tier payouts, grants, insurance premiums, or Family Bank lending under any circumstance.
🏆
Merit-Based Participation. Distribution eligibility is earned annually through documented financial behavior, family contribution, and compliance. It can be lost.
🌱
Youth Protection. Ages 16-20 may qualify for tier placement, but all distributions are restricted to investment, education, and insurance channels. No cash payments.
🤝
Family Unity Above All. Tier placements are strictly confidential. Anti-shaming rules and structured dispute resolution prevent family-destroying dynamics.
⚖️
Non-Dilution is Mandatory. No program expands beneficiary rights beyond the governing instrument. Program participation does not create beneficiary status unless already defined.

Section IV, Scaling Policy

Administration Scaling Policy

As corpus grows and operations mature, the Administration percentage scales downward. Scaling rewards growth: as the Trust grows larger, more resources flow to reinvestment, not operations. Reduced admin percentages redirect savings to Growth/Retained only.

Corpus LevelAdmin TargetNotes
Below $3M15%Default administration level
$3M, $6M13%Efficiency gains expected. Maintain audit/legal buffers
$6M, $10M11-12%Range determined annually based on operational complexity
$10M and above10%Mature operations. Emphasis on automation and standardized reviews
Scaling adjustments do not increase the Tier Pool above 50% absent a formal amendment. Reduced Admin percentages redirect savings to Growth/Retained only.

Section V, Governance

Trustee Structure & Decision Rights

The Trust is governed by a three-person Board: one Independent Trustee (non-family professional) and two Family Trustees with staggered terms. The Independent Trustee serves as the structural safeguard against corpus raids, inconsistent rule application, and family-interest conflicts. Trustee compensation is capped at 2% of annual yield, paid from Administration only.

🗳️

Unanimous Vote Required

Allocation % changes, tier rule changes, Family Bank loans above 5% corpus, loan forgiveness, and any action that could impair corpus integrity.

✅

Majority Vote (2 of 3)

Standard Family Bank loans within limits, routine summit budget approvals, vendor selection, and tier placement confirmations after scoring committee review.

All decisions require written minutes stored in the Trust governance repository. Verbal decisions carry no weight and must be ratified in writing within 15 days.
Annual Operating Cycle
Q1
Close Books & Allocate
Close prior-year books, calculate net yield (Y), apply the 15/35/50 split, set annual caps and committee calendar.
Q2
Score & Place Members
Collect documentation packets, conduct scoring, preliminary tier placements, publish confidential tier decisions to each member.
Q3
Execute Distributions
Execute Tier distributions and Family Bank decisions. Withhold and pay approved insurance premiums.
Q4
Family Summit & Review
Hold the Family Summit and annual governance review. Capture improvement opportunities for the next-year cycle.

Section VII, Tier System

Section IX, Insurance

Mandatory Insurance Requirement

Insurance coverage is a condition of distribution participation, not an option. All eligible members receiving tier distributions must carry a minimum required policy. The premium is withheld directly from the member's distribution before any cash or directed payment is made.

"Distributions are not suspended, they are reduced by the withheld premium amount."

📝

Insurability Exception

Members unable to obtain coverage due to documented medical issues may apply for a waiver. Requires two written carrier declinations plus a trustee-approved alternative. Waivers are documented exceptions, not penalties, and do not reduce tier scoring.

⚰️

Death Benefit Flow

Mandatory minimum policy payout flows to Trust corpus, strengthening the principal base. Additional Multiplier coverage payout also flows to Trust corpus unless a trustee-approved rider designates a split for surviving dependents.

Section IX (Continued), Multiplier

Insurance Multiplier Program

The Insurance Multiplier Program rewards members who go beyond the mandatory minimum, carrying additional coverage that further strengthens the Trust's long-term capital position. The Multiplier applies exclusively to verified coverage above the 3x baseline.

The mandatory minimum policy (3x income) earns no tier score bonus. The Multiplier Program begins where the minimum ends. Additional coverage beyond 3x qualifies for tier score boosts, capped at +15%.

Section XI, Grants

Trust Formation Grant Program (TFGP)

The TFGP exists to help eligible extended family members establish independent trust structures, strengthening governance culture across the broader family ecosystem without diluting The Sunshine Trust.

TFGP grants are funded exclusively from the 35% Growth/Retained Earnings allocation. Never from the Tier Pool, never from Administration, and never from corpus. This is non-negotiable.
📏

Annual Grant Limits

Total annual TFGP grants may not exceed 25% of the annual Growth allocation. Individual grant may not exceed 10% of the annual Growth allocation. If insufficient Growth allocation exists, grants are deferred, never borrowed from other buckets.

🚫

Non-Dilution Protection

TFGP recipients gain zero rights in The Sunshine Trust: no beneficiary rights, no distribution rights from any bucket, no governance authority, no voting rights, and no access to Trust financial information beyond grant documentation.

Section XII, Family Bank

The Family Bank Program

The Family Bank is a structured, interest-bearing internal lending facility designed to support productive uses: business formation, real estate, education. Family Bank loans are funded exclusively from Growth/Retained reserves. No Tier Pool, no Administration, and absolutely no corpus.

"APR: Prime Rate minus 1.50%, with a floor at the current Applicable Federal Rate (AFR). All interest payments return to Growth/Retained and compound the corpus over time."

Section XIV, Enforcement

Enforcement & Dispute Resolution

🚨

Enforcement Actions

Misrepresentation/fraud: immediate ineligibility, potential legal action, permanent bar from all programs. Missed documentation deadline: automatic tier suspension, no retroactive reinstatement. Harassment or tier disclosure: formal discipline up to removal. Loan default: tier suspension triggered at 90 days.

🕊️

Dispute Resolution

Step 1: Informal direct resolution between parties. Step 2: Neutral third-party mediation by mutual consent. Step 3: Formal written Trustee Board ruling. Step 4: Legal counsel only if trustee decision is challenged.

"The Trust prioritizes family unity. Disputes should be resolved at the lowest level possible. Escalation to legal counsel is a last resort, not a first response."

Non-Dilution Standard (Strict): No program participation expands beneficiary status. Beneficiary status may only be created through a formal amendment. Reclassification of any person as a beneficiary without a proper amendment is void and of no legal effect.

Section XV, Culture & Philosophy

Values, Culture & Generational Covenant

The Sunshine Trust is more than a legal structure. It is a family philosophy in motion. The governance systems, allocation rules, and tier mechanics exist to express and reinforce a set of deeply held values about money, family, and legacy.

🛡️
Principal is sacred and protected. We do not consume what produces our abundance.
🏆
Yield is earned through stewardship, not entitlement.
🤝
Participation is voluntary and dignified. No one is compelled. No one is shamed.
🚫
No entitlement mindset. Benefits are merit-based and earned annually.
🔒
No public comparisons or rankings. Tier information is private, always.
❤️
Unity is our #1 priority. The Trust exists to bring the family together, not to create hierarchy.
⚙️
We build systems, not dependencies. Every program is designed to make members more capable, not more reliant.

"We use money as a tool, not as identity. We protect principal while deploying yield. We build cultural infrastructure for future generations. We create disciplined systems that outlive us."

Every decision made by the current generation echoes forward in time. The choices we make today become the foundation our children and grandchildren build upon. We do not build the Trust for today. We build it for the generation that hasn't been born yet.
